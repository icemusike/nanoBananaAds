# Landing Page Frameworks

## High-Converting Landing Page Structures

### 1. The Hero-First Framework (Modern SaaS)
**Best for**: Software, Apps, Tech Products
**Conversion Rate**: 15-25% average

**Structure**:
1. **Hero Section** (Above fold)
   - Power headline (benefit + outcome)
   - Sub-headline (mechanism)
   - Primary CTA button
   - Hero image/video
   - Trust indicators (logos, ratings)

2. **Problem Section**
   - 3-5 pain points with icons
   - "Without our solution" narrative

3. **Solution Section**
   - "How it works" (3-step process)
   - Features as benefits (icon + headline + description)

4. **Social Proof**
   - Testimonials with photos
   - Case studies with metrics
   - Logo wall of customers

5. **Pricing/Offer**
   - Clear pricing table or CTA
   - Value stack
   - Money-back guarantee

6. **Final CTA**
   - Recap benefits
   - Strong action button
   - Last objection handler

---

### 2. The Long-Form Sales Letter (High-Ticket)
**Best for**: Courses, Coaching, Premium Products
**Conversion Rate**: 3-8% (but higher value)

**Structure**:
1. **Pattern Interrupt**
   - Bold claim or story hook
   - "Warning" or "Attention" pre-headline

2. **Headline + Sub-headline**
   - Big promise with specificity
   - Unique mechanism tease

3. **Story/Origin**
   - Founder's journey
   - Discovery of mechanism
   - Build credibility

4. **Pain Agitation** (Extended)
   - Current frustrations (5-7 points)
   - Cost of inaction
   - Failed alternatives

5. **Solution Introduction**
   - Name the mechanism
   - How it's different
   - Why it works

6. **Proof Bomb**
   - Multiple case studies
   - Before/after transformations
   - Testimonial videos

7. **What You Get**
   - Core offer
   - Bonus stack (with individual values)
   - Total value vs. price

8. **Risk Reversal**
   - Strong guarantee
   - Why you can trust us

9. **Urgency/Scarcity**
   - Limited spots/time
   - Price increase date
   - Countdown timer

10. **FAQ**
    - 8-12 common objections
    - Build desire while handling concerns

11. **Final Pitch**
    - Emotional recap
    - Clear next step
    - Multiple CTAs

---

### 3. The VSL + Order Form (Video Sales Letter)
**Best for**: Information Products, Memberships
**Conversion Rate**: 5-12%

**Structure**:
1. **Video Player** (Centered, large)
   - Auto-play or click-to-play
   - No scrubbing (for webinars)
   - Subtitles available

2. **Minimal Distractions**
   - Remove navigation
   - Hide header/footer until video watched

3. **Order Button** (Appears after key moment)
   - Sticky or appears at CTA points
   - Bright, contrasting color

4. **Trust Elements** (Below video)
   - Money-back guarantee badge
   - Secure payment icons
   - Customer count/testimonials

5. **FAQ Accordion** (Below order form)
   - Brief answers
   - Link to full FAQ if needed

---

### 4. The Product Launch Formula (PLF)
**Best for**: Product Launches, Courses, Software Launches
**Conversion Rate**: 10-20% (during launch window)

**Pre-Launch Sequence**:
- Video 1: The Opportunity (identify desire)
- Video 2: The Transformation (show what's possible)
- Video 3: The Ownership Experience (how it works)

**Launch Day Structure**:
1. **Announcement**
   - "We're Open" headline
   - Limited cart open time
   - Countdown timer

2. **Quick Recap**
   - What you learned in pre-launch
   - Benefits summary

3. **The Offer**
   - Core product
   - Launch bonuses (time-sensitive)
   - Price + payment options

4. **Social Proof Wall**
   - Real-time testimonials
   - "People are buying" notifications

5. **Urgent CTA**
   - Cart closes message
   - Final warning
   - No re-opens (set expectation)

---

### 5. The Simple Squeeze Page (Lead Generation)
**Best for**: Free Offers, Lead Magnets, Webinar Registration
**Conversion Rate**: 25-50%

**Structure**:
1. **Headline** (Clear benefit)
   - "Get [Specific Result]"
   - No fluff, just outcome

2. **3-5 Bullet Points**
   - What they'll learn/get
   - Each bullet = one desire

3. **Opt-in Form**
   - Name + Email only
   - Large, obvious submit button
   - Privacy statement

4. **Social Proof** (Optional)
   - "Join 10,000+ marketers"
   - Single testimonial

5. **Trust Elements**
   - No spam promise
   - What happens next

**Keep it minimal** - remove ALL distractions

---

### 6. The Webinar Registration Page
**Best for**: High-ticket offers, Expert positioning
**Conversion Rate**: 30-60% registration

**Structure**:
1. **Pre-headline**
   - "Free training" or "Live masterclass"

2. **Headline**
   - "How to [Get Desired Result]"
   - Include timeframe if compelling

3. **Sub-headline**
   - "(Even if [Common objection])"

4. **What You'll Learn** (3-4 bullets)
   - Numbered list
   - Each point = mini-headline

5. **Date/Time Selector**
   - Multiple time options
   - Auto-timezone detection
   - "Choose your session" CTA

6. **Host Credibility**
   - Photo + bio
   - Credentials/results
   - "Who this is for"

7. **Testimonials** (2-3)
   - From past attendees
   - Video preferred

8. **Registration Form**
   - Name + Email
   - Phone (optional)
   - Submit = "Save My Spot"

## Conversion Elements (Universal)

### Above the Fold Must-Haves
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Primary CTA visible
- [ ] Hero visual (image/video)
- [ ] Trust indicator

### Throughout the Page
- [ ] White space (not cluttered)
- [ ] Benefit-driven copy
- [ ] Social proof every 2-3 sections
- [ ] Multiple CTAs (every 1.5 screens)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized layout
- [ ] Fast load time (<3 seconds)

### Psychological Triggers
- **Scarcity**: Limited quantity/time
- **Urgency**: Countdown, deadline
- **Social Proof**: Testimonials, numbers
- **Authority**: Credentials, media logos
- **Reciprocity**: Free value upfront
- **Consistency**: Pre-commitment questions

---

## Framework Selection Guide

**Choose based on**:
- **Price point**: <$100 = Short form | $100-500 = Medium | $500+ = Long form
- **Awareness level**: Aware = Short | Unaware = Long
- **Product complexity**: Simple = Short | Complex = Long + video
- **Sales cycle**: Impulse = Short | Considered = Long

**When in doubt**: Start with Hero-First Framework for most products.
